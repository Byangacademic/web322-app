Massive Open Worlds: MMOs are known for their vast and immersive open worlds. From sprawling fantasy realms to futuristic sci-fi cities, these virtual landscapes are filled with diverse environments, including lush forests, treacherous dungeons, towering mountains, and bustling cities. Exploring these worlds and uncovering hidden secrets is a thrilling aspect of MMO gameplay.

Social Interaction: MMOs are built around social interaction, enabling players to connect with each other in a virtual community. Whether it's joining guilds, participating in player-run events, or simply chatting with fellow adventurers, MMOs foster a sense of camaraderie and cooperation among players.

Player vs. Environment (PvE): MMOs often offer a rich PvE experience, where players can embark on epic quests, defeat challenging monsters and bosses, and unravel deep storylines. PvE content can range from solo adventures to group-based raids, requiring coordination and teamwork to overcome powerful adversaries.
